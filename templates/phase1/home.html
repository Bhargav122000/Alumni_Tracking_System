Welcome to the - Alumni Tracking System


Our website is helpful in order to maintain to a dynamic and updated central platform for the tracking of alumni.

In current scenario, the database of Alumni is maintained by college which is static. Due to this reason, the real time information of alumni is not present on any central platform. A platform must exist where all the alumni can easily update their profile, have persistent storage of student data and can be easily accessed by college.

Our website provides following facilities:

  • Provision for Users(both Alumni members & Institutions) to register and login.
  • Provision for Alumni members to create , view their profile(personal, educational, professional) and update if necessary.
  • Provision for alumni members to save their profile on central platform(where respective institutions can track them).
  • Provision for Institutions to view their alumni, (view as a whole, or by some criteria).
  • Communication facility between alumni and institutions by messaging through mails.
  • Create events on the portal.
